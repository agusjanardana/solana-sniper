# Solana Meme Sniper Bot

Production-ready Solana meme coin sniper bot with:
- Backtesting
- Paper Trading
- Live Trading
- Scam Filtering
- TP/SL Automation
- Risk Management
- Realtime Dashboard
- Notifications

---

# Objectives

The system should:
- Detect new meme coin opportunities
- Filter scam/rug tokens
- Enter trades automatically
- Manage positions automatically
- Exit quickly with TP/SL
- Support safe testing before live trading
- Be modular and scalable

---

# Development Stages
